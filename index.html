Welcome to Kasi Bistro

At Kasi Bistro, we bring the heartbeat of the township to your plate.Founded in 2024
and proudly based in Polokwane, we're more than just a restaurant
— we're a celebration of flavour, culture, and community.
We fuse classic high-end cuisine with a proudly local twist,
creating bold dishes crafted for the South African palate. Our menu is inspired by the streets, traditions, and people who make South Africa beautiful.
Whether you're joining us for a quick lunch, a chilled evening with friends, or a festive celebration,
you'll find a vibe that feels like home. Local ingredients, bold spices, and authentic recipes come ,
together to create unforgettable meals that tell a story. But Kasi Bistro isn't just about food — it's about connection. We're a space where good music plays,
laughter echoes, and memories are made. Our team is here to make you feel welcome, whether you're a ,
first-time visitor or part of the Kasi Bistro family. Explore our menu, follow us on socials, or book your next event with us. We also offer delivery,
catering, and weekly specials that keep things fresh and exciting. So come through — relax, eat well, and experience kasi culture on a plate.
